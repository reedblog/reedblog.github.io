Work

My work focuses on using statistics to improve public health programs. In my current job as an epidemiologist, I conduct studies of environmental chemical exposure among Washington State residents.

In addition to public health, I'm a strong proponent of open science and evidence-based policy. My long-term goals are to better understand how social and environmental factors influence the distribution of disease in society, and to use that understanding to advocate for healthy policies and health equity.
